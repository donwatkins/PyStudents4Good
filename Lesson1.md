### At the REPL enter the following expressions
* 2 + 3
* 2 * 3 
* 3/5
* 2 **4
### What happened? 
----
### Order of operations
* '+' = Addition

* '-' = Subtraction

* '/' = Division

* '*' = Multiplication

* '**' = Exponent

* '//' = Integer division

* '%' = Modulus remainder
----
